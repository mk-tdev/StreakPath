# Privacy Policy for StreakPath

**Last Updated: December 30, 2024**

## Introduction

StreakPath ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Habit Data**: Habit names, icons, colors, groups, and completion records
- **User Name** (Optional): If you provide a name during guest login

### Automatically Collected Data
- None. We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Local Storage**: All your habit data is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your data is never transmitted to our servers or third parties
- **No Analytics**: We do not track your usage or behavior

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase Authentication
We use Firebase Authentication for future Google Sign-in functionality. When you use Google Sign-in (coming soon):
- Firebase may collect authentication data per [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- We do not have access to your Google account credentials

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data (visible in the app)
- Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
- Export your data (no current export feature)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
